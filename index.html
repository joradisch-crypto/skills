Step 1 of 2 — Backend Setup

Connect a Storage Bin

This app uses JSONBin.io (free) to store responses so your whole team shares the same data from any device.

⚠️ You only do this once. After setup, just share your GitHub Pages URL and everyone connects automatically.

Quick Setup (2 minutes)

1
Go to jsonbin.io and create a free account (just email + password).
2
After logging in, click "+ Create Bin", paste in {"members":[],"responses":{}} as the content, and click Save. Copy the Bin ID from the URL (it looks like 64a3f...).
3
Go to your Account → API Keys and copy your Access Key.
4
Paste both below and click Save.
🔒 Privacy note

Your API key and bin ID are stored only in your browser's localStorage — they never leave your device except to talk to JSONBin directly. The bin itself is private by default on JSONBin.
